The Target Audience:

Person's of any age from all walks of life that wish to get accurate weather information.

Persona 1:

  • Name: Johnny Boy Scout
  • Age: 12
  • Goals: Research before an outing.
  • Environment: Android

Persona 2:

  • Name: Ethel and Frank
  • Age: 88 and 91
  • Goals: Check to see if jacket required for daily walk.
  • Environment: Desktop computer

Scenarios:

  1. Check weather before make a long drive.
  2. Confirm that the day will be good for a picnic.
  3. Vacation planning.
  4. Yardwork.
  5. See if bet won from last week!
